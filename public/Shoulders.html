Back & Shoulder exercises


Barbell Shoulder Press

Instructions
    Stand with feet shoulder width, tighten core, with Barbell in hands, palms facing out, rested on upper-chest/ shoulder blades. Push the bar upward and squeeze shoulder blades together at peak. Lower steadily.

Seated Dumbell Shoulder Press

Instructions
    Sit on bench with dumbbells in both hands, at shoulder level, palms facing out. Keeping back completely straight, push dumbbells upward and toward each other. When at peak, reverse course, slow and steadily.

Weight Plate Front Raise

Instructions
    Stand with weighted plate in hands at hip height, slowly lift weight to shoulder level with straight arms, lower and repeat.

Dumbell Lateral Raise

Instructions
    Stand with dumbbells in both hands, with arms at your side, slowly raise arms wide to make a ‘T’ position, and lower down.

Barbell Hip Thrust

Instructions
    Sitting to the side of a bench, facing out, with elbows on bench, and barbell at your hips, grip barbell with hands and thrust upwards with hips to where your body is parallel with ground, and knees at 90-degree angle. Let down slowly.

Bent-Over Dumbell Lateral Raise

Instructions
    Stand with dumbbells in both hands; keeping back straight, bend over until your core is almost parallel with ground, hang dumbbells underneath you, and slowly raise dumbbells out to make a ‘T’ with upper body. Let down slowly to hang beneath you again.

Barbell Shrugs

Instructions
    Stand with barbell at waist-level, in both hands; lift shoulders up and back (shrug), keeping the movements restricted to your shoulders and neck (no arm movements).

Wide-Grip Pull Up

Instructions
    Using a pull up bar (or any bar wide enough), grip wide, as to where arms are at 90 degrees, pull yourself up as to where chin is level with or above the bar.

Deadlift

Instructions
    Stand in-front of bar with feet shoulder width apart, bend knees, keeping back ‘locked’, and straight, and chest up, grip the bar and pull up ,with straight arms, digging your feet into the ground, until you are standing with the bar at your hips. Drop the bar down or reverse movements, keeping back straight, and lay bar back down to the ground.

Barbell Row

Instructions
    Stand in front of barbell, with feet shoulder width, bend forward to where core is parallel with ground; grip barbell, pull to chest slowly whilst keeping back rigid and straight. Lower down and repeat. Done rest bar back on ground.

Chest Supported Row

Instructions
    Lie face down on bench elevate 30 degrees, with dumbbells in hands, hanging underneath you; bring dumbbells into chest slowly and let down again.

Dumbell Row

Instructions
    Kneel on bench with one arm and same sided knee, with other leg standing rigid, and dumbbell in free hand; let the dumbbell hang, then slowly bring dumbbell up to chest, and slowly let down to hang again.

Lateral Pulldown

Instructions
    Sat on box or bench, under Cable Pulley Machine (or pull-up bar, if using a strong resistance band), use a wide grip, where arms are making a 90 degrees angle, and pull down to where bar is at eye level, or to where you can not pull the resistance band anymore, in a controlled manner.

Renegade Dumbell row

Instructions
    Get into push up position, whilst gripping dumbbells in both hands; using alternative hands, bring dumbbell up to hip and let back down to rest.

YTW

Instructions
    Lie on bench face down, with dumbbells in either hand, hanging down; in a controlled manner, make a ‘Y’ with your upper body, raising arms at a 45 degree angle, then proceed to make a ‘T’ with your upper body, raising arms straight out, and then proceed to bring your arms into a 90 degree angle at the elbow, and raise up to make a ‘W’ with your arms and head, then bring to rest.
